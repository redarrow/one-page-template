Our Lawn Care Services in Mahoning County

Turftime Lawn Care provides a variety of services to keep your lawn looking its best in Mahoning County, including areas like Youngstown, Boardman, Austintown, and Canfield. Our skilled team offers everything from routine mowing to landscape design, catering to all your lawn care needs.

Lawn Mowing & Maintenance

Regular lawn mowing, edging, and general upkeep to keep your lawn looking neat and tidy.

Fertilization & Weed Control

Application of fertilizers to promote healthy grass growth and treatments to eliminate and prevent weeds.

Shrub & Hedge Trimming

Pruning and shaping of shrubs and hedges to maintain their health and appearance.

Leaf Removal & Cleanup

Seasonal leaf removal and general yard cleanup to keep your property clean and attractive.

Aeration & Overseeding

Aerating the soil to allow air, water, and nutrients to reach the roots, followed by overseeding to promote a lush, full lawn.

Landscape Design & Installation

Custom design and installation of landscaping features, including flower beds, mulch, and decorative elements.
